PhD Student, Deep Learning, IIST- Thiruvananthapuram · r4ramzan@gmail.com

I am a PhD student in the Department of Avionics at the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology Thiruvananthapuram. I work on the topic of seizure detection from EEG data. My work focuses on multivariate time-series analysis using state space models and graph neural networks for the seizure detection task.
