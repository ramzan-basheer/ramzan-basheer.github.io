Focus Areas

Research Interests

Graphs

Equivariant Graph Neural Networks

Multi-edge and hypergraph architectures with Z₂ hemispheric symmetry constraints for anatomically consistent EEG modelling. Group-equivariant convolutions that respect the bilateral structure of the brain and generalise across patients without retraining.

Geometry

Riemannian & Manifold Methods

SPD matrix spaces with Log-Euclidean metrics for covariance-based EEG feature extraction. Fréchet mean aggregation, geodesic operations, and SPD manifold closure properties. Extends standard Euclidean deep learning to non-flat curved feature spaces.

Sequences

State Space Models

S4 and Mamba selective state space models for long-range EEG sequences of 12,000 steps at 200 Hz. Linear-time complexity with global receptive fields. Integrated into multi-edge graph pipelines via the Hypercube-S4GNN architecture for joint temporal and spatial modelling.

EEG · Clinical

Epileptic Seizure Detection & Prediction

End-to-end graph-based multivariate EEG pipelines for epileptic event detection and anticipation. Validated on TUSZ v1.5.2 (detection) and CHB-MIT / Siena Scalp EEG (prediction) with leave-one-seizure-out and cross-session protocols. Target: real-time, patient-adaptable clinical deployment.
